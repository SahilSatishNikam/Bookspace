Histroy Of Books

The history of books is a journey of human knowledge and creativity that began around 3000 BCE with clay tablets in Mesopotamia. Ancient Egyptians later developed papyrus scrolls, while Greeks and Romans advanced the use of parchment and codices, which were easier to handle. During the Middle Ages, books were handwritten often decorated with beautiful illustrations by monks in monasteries. The invention of paper in China around the 2nd century CE further revolutionized bookmaking. A major transformation occurred in the 15th century when Johannes Gutenberg introduced the printing press, making books affordable and accessible to the masses. science, and literature across Europe. Over centuries, printed books became the foundation of libraries and learning worldwide. In the 20th century, the introduction of paperback editions made reading even more convenient. Today, books exist not only in print but also in digital formats like e-books and audiobooks.
