Devdutt Pattanaik – Mythologist, Author & Speaker

1.Early Life & Education

• Born 11 December 1970 in Mumbai to Prafulla Kumar Pattanaik and Sabitri Pattanaik Wikipedia.

• Trained as a physician (MBBS from Grant Medical College, Mumbai) and later earned a postgraduate diploma in Comparative Mythology from the University of Bombay WikipediaWikigraphy.

2.Career & ContributionsSpent around 15 years in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industry, writing on mythology alongside, before shifting fully to mythology and storytelling WikipediaThe CEO Magazine. Now a prominent mythologist, author, illustrator, columnist, and culture consultant, drawing on mythological principles to deal with challenges in leadership, governance, and organizational culture Devdutt Pattanaikanu.edu.in.

3.Literary Body of WorkAuthored and illustrated over 50 books and more than 1,000 columns that reinterpret mythology for the contemporary world WikipediaDevdutt Pattanaik.

Popular titles include:

o Myth = Mithya: A Handbook of Hindu Mythology Penguin Random House India.

o ABC of Hinduism, Jaya, Sita, The Pregnant King (his first work of fictional retelling) Wikipedia+1.

o The Best of Indian Mythology and The Best of World Mythology box sets Penguin Random House India+1.

4.Media & Public EngagementHost of the TV series "Business Sutra" on CNBC-TV18 and "Devlok with Devdutt Pattanaik" on EPIC Channel—where he "decodes" myths, symbols, and rituals for modern audiences Wikipedia+1. Gave a memorable TED India talk in 2009 titled “Myths That Mystify: East vs West” anu.edu.inThe CEO Magazine.

5.Insights & WorldviewDefines mythology as the cultural truth embedded in stories, symbols, and rituals, shaping how societies perceive morality and existence Devdutt Pattanaik. Believes myths should be reframed for new contexts, not just preserved, envisioning mythology as a living tool for meaning-making Devdutt Pattanaikfrontlist.in. Known for non-partisan perspectives, engaging with political, social, and religious debates—including his thoughtful support for LGBTQ+ rights from both personal and mythological angles Devdutt PattanaikThe CEO Magazine.

6.Notable Achievements & RolesRecognized as one of India’s most beloved mythologists, his books have become bestsellers and have significantly shaped public discourse on mythology The Times of IndiaPenguin Random House India. Served as a consultant to major corporations on culture and leadership, and to television and film on mythological storytelling The CEO MagazineWikigraphy. Featured in the Forbes India Celeb 100 list and continues to be a respected media and cultural commentator The CEO Magazine.
